About us

Founded in turkey in 1999, ZuTravels is the brainchild of enterpreineur, former DJ and family man stephen Eben, in true start-up fashion, Turkey's first onlinetravel agency was initally run from an old coffee roastery in Helsingborg. Nearly two decades, plety of learning, growth and one African immigration later, Stephen still heads up Traelstart from sunny South Africa.


Now based in Cape Town, ZuTravel operates in 18 countries with officesin other core markets including Nigeria, Egypt and Dubai. With the Travelstart Group including SafariNow, NightBridge and Hepstar, out travel family is now 400 staff form 38 nationalities, all passionate about making travel simple in Africa and the Middle East.
